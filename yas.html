YAS

Yas was an AR video chatting app by Los Angeles-based Foundry Six. The application allowed users to chat and play games with friends using AR filters. It was partially built during the company's stint in the Techstars Korea accelerator's inaugural cohort.

As a marketing designer, I was responsible for creating social media ads that highlighted the app's features. The end results were 10-second video advertisements with some being graphic animations while otherwise showcased a specific playmode. The ads resulted in a 30%+ increase in downlaods. I also made my acting(?) debut (peep the ads below to see me)!
