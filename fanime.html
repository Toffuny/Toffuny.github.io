FanimeCon

Branding
2017 - 2018

Fanime is an annual anime convention aimed to gather anime fans from all walks of life. It includes everything to bring fans of the same shows together from artist booths and video game competitions to cosplay gatherings. Each year has a different theme that influences what special events will be planned that year (2018's theme was gaming)

As an environmental brand designer, I took part in designing how the event space would look like, what assets would be needed, and what the assets would actually be.

  • Printing + preparing signage for display
