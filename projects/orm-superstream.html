Superstream site design

OVERVIEW
O'Reilly Media offers a series of live virtual events on their online learning platform called Superstreams. Each Superstream focuses on a different topic with designated hosts for each one.
SCOPE OF WORK
Web design, UI/UX
COMPANY
O'Reilly Media
YEAR
2024
THE CHALLENGE
The Superstream events have grown in number and variety since the original event conception to now cover over 10+ different topics. There became a need to revamp the branding to fit the scope, look, and feel we want users to gain when looking to attend one of the events.
THE RESULT
The final look was one that came from a desire to show the sleekness and modernity of how O'Reilly Media events are; it's still the animal book company, but we've also evolved as the technology we're teaching has.
The team and I decided that it would be a more efficient use of everyone's time to create a webpage displaying all the information. A webpage would be easier to apply changes to and it'd also eliminate the worry of outdated information being spread around. I helped in building the main web page providing information and listings of upcoming events as well as designing the mobile version of the experience.
