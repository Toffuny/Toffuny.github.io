2017

Tiffany Truong's personal logo.
It combines the "T" that begins both her first and last name as well as Tiffany's Chinese surname "張." The left side represents the past that she relys upon while the right sans serif side represents modern times. The simplistic logo reflects the belief that the past and future are connected, what happens in the past will always play a role in the future.

Water Arm (2018)

This prosthetic attachs onto one's dislocated shoulder to serve as an upgraded replacement to someone's old arm. This arm has the ability to pull water molecules from the body and use them to generate water, ice, or steam. The panel located on the upper arm shows the user's current water levels and automatically stops drawing molecules.
