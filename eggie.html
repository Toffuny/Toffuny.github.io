Eggie

Eggie is an online fashion store created by Youtube personality Jenn Im. The store specializes in clothing for young women and offers a variety of styles to choose from.
Done as part of a 24-hour group creative jam.

Objective: 24 hours to select and redesign a website of our choosing while still maintaining the brand's identity.
Role: Designer
Tools:Figma
Deliverables: Logo, website templates (Originals on the left, proposed designs on the right)
Outcome: 4 redesigns of site pages are proposed that maintain the site's simple, minimalistic approach but aim to add more color and structure. In addition, a new section of the store dubbed "The Gram," a reference to Im's occupation as an influencer, is a feature like Likeshop wherein anybody who tags photos of how they style Eggie's products with Eggie's instagram handle(@eggie.us) will pop up on this section of the site. The feature allows site visitors to look at outfit inspiration and be directed to specific items, thus expediting the shopping process.
A new logo is also proposed to align with the new pages.
