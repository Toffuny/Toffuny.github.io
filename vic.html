Call of Duty Mobile

Call of Duty Mobile is a game app that brings the beloved game into the palms of your hands. An ad campaign was constructed to draw more attention to the game's initial release by using and editing actual gameplay footage from the app itself.
Objective: Delivery a number of advertisements for the client every month to help boost engagement and game downloads. Highlight beloved game maps and weapons of the console version as they appear in the app.
Role: Video Editor
Outcome: Participated in a successful ad campaign that resulted in an increase of traffic to the game in the App Store and higher ad engagements.
Deliverables: Short (max 30 second) video advertisements showing features and gameplay shown across various platforms (In-app ads, Facebook, YouTube, Twitter)
