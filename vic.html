Roof on Fire (屋顶着火) Album

Objective: Redesign an album of my choice so that each component's design is all related.
Role: Designer
Outcome: Proposed an album design for Chinese idol Song Qian's "Roof on Fire." The concept is based on the lyrics, which speak about joining hands with another just like her to just do what they want (as it's easy to make mistakes at night).
Deliverables: CD design concept
