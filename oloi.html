OLOI

Oloi is a motorsports inspired apparel brand dedicated to creating high quality clothes one can wear on and off the track. The company also sponsors F3 Asia racers in partnership with EVANS GP

As the Designer/Website Manager, I spent the bulk of my days operating the website. This includes updating and customizing the website in accordance to new releases or promotions as well as screening orders. The last site redesign I spearheaded (below) was in line with the release of the new touring collection. Highlighting the clothes' functionality in daily life while keeping the rest simple (to not overload visitors with too much sensory overload) was the main priority. The site reflects the clothes' ability to do so much without being too much.
