Grill?

Video

2018

In the growing age of eSports, more and more people of all types and walks of life are queueing up for games. Female identifying people still make up the minority of the player game of many popular franchises and are unfortunately being harassed solely based on their gender. Grill? offers a glimpse into what they deal with on a daily basis when trying to play something they enjoy.
